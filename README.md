# 🍽️ PlateShare – Community Food Sharing Platform

**Live Site:** https://your-live-site-link.com

PlateShare is a community-based food sharing platform where people can donate their surplus food and help reduce food waste. Users can easily post food items they want to share, and others can browse available foods and request them when needed. The goal of this platform is to connect communities and ensure that extra food reaches people who need it instead of being wasted.

---

##  Key Features

*  **User Authentication**
  Secure authentication system using Firebase with Email/Password login and Google login.
*  **Food Donation System**
  Logged-in users can add food items with details such as food name, quantity, pickup location, and expiry date.

*  **Available Food Listing**
  All available food items are displayed in a clean card layout where users can explore and view details.

*  **Food Request System**
  Users can request food from the details page, and the food owner can accept or reject requests.

*  **Manage My Foods**
  Donators can update or delete the foods they have added from a dedicated management page.

*  **My Food Requests Page**
  Users can track the foods they have requested.

*  **Dynamic Featured Foods Section**
  The homepage shows featured foods dynamically based on the highest quantity.

*  **Responsive & Clean UI**
  Fully responsive design for mobile, tablet, and desktop with consistent UI components.
